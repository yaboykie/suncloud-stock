How Your Pricing Works
Discount
Applied at time of purchase
Your discount is taken off the RRP on every order. This is your buy price from Suncloud — the price on your invoice.
Volume Rebate
Credit applied to next month's order
Based on your monthly order volume, you earn a rebate as a credit on the following month's invoice. The more you order, the more you save next month.
Example: $10,000 monthly orders at 5% discount + 5% rebate
January
Order: $10,000
5% discount: −$500
You pay: $9,500
5% rebate ($475) earned →
February
Order: $10,000
5% discount: −$500
Jan rebate credit: −$475
You pay: $9,025
5% rebate ($475) earned →
March & Ongoing
Order: $10,000
5% discount: −$500
Feb rebate credit: −$475
You pay: $9,025
Repeats every month ✓
From month 2 onwards, your effective cost per $10,000 order is $9,025 — a combined 9.75% off RRP.

How the Rebate Works
Suncloud Energy Volume Rebate Program
The short version
Your discount is applied on every invoice, always. The rebate is separate — it’s a credit applied to next month’s invoice based on your total monthly order volume. The rebate is not a discount on the same invoice.
How It Works
1
You order at your discounted price
Every invoice is issued at your agreed discount off RRP. This is the amount you pay on the invoice.
2
End of month — Suncloud tallies your total
If your total monthly orders hit the rebate threshold, you qualify for the rebate on that month’s spend.
3
Rebate credit appears on next month’s invoice
The rebate is not a discount on the same invoice — it’s a dollar credit applied to your next order. This lowers your effective cost per system over time.
Discount is applied at invoice. Rebate is credited to the following month’s order — not the same invoice. Rebate applies to your total monthly spend across all product lines. Confirm your discount and rebate rates with your Suncloud State Manager.
